About VIT-AP University

Est 1984

Our Story

With a rich legacy of 39 years of innovation in education and research, VIT has established itself as a leader in delivering quality education. Consistently ranked among the top educational institutions in the country, the VIT group takes pride in achieving new heights of knowledge and excellence through hard work and dedication. Continuing this tradition, the pioneering leadership at VIT-AP University fosters strong academia-industry partnerships, driving forward with decisiveness and dynamism to become a leading university. VIT-AP University offers a wide range of opportunities and training programs to help students explore their abilities, develop core competencies, and undergo a holistic transformation to become better global citizens, embodying the motto 'Apply Knowledge - Improve Life.'

About the Department of Physics

Established in 2017 with a modest start, the Department of Physics has expanded to include 25 expert physicists. We offer an M.Sc. in Physics with a focus on Energy Science and Technology and a Ph.D. program featuring a three-year fellowship. Our scholars receive financial support for various needs, including contingency funds, publication fees, and conference travel.

The School of Advanced Sciences strives to be a Centre of Excellence, impacting society through education, research, and innovation.

Solid Foundation

We provide students with a solid foundation in engineering physics and practical laboratory experience.

Practical Laboratory Experience

Laboratory training complements classroom learning, enhancing students' knowledge of experimental design.

Research Collaboration

Students have opportunities to collaborate on research projects with faculty, equipping them with critical analytical and problem-solving skills for future technological challenges.

About i-ReAD MAGMA 24'

The i-ReAD MAGMA 24 conference aims to bring together researchers, scientists, and industry professionals working in the field of magnetism and magnetic materials. Participants will have the opportunity to exchange knowledge, discuss recent developments, and explore collaborative opportunities. The conference spans various domains, making it relevant for undergraduates, postgraduates, Ph.D. scholars, and faculty members alike.

Fundamentals of magnetism

  • Skyrmions
  • Frustrated magnetism
  • Topological insulators
  • Spintronics

Functional materials

  • Multiferroics
  • Magnetoelectric effect and applications
  • Magnetic cooling technologies: Magnetocaloric materials
  • Ferrofluids

Applied magnetism

  • Magnetic hyperthermia and cancer therapy
  • Magnetic materials for energy and devices
  • Giant magnetoresistance, Tunnel magnetoresistance
  • High-Density magnetic recording technologies
  • Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI)
  • Magnetic particles in biotechnology and medicine
  • Magnetic field-Based energy harvesting
  • Magnetic enhancements in battery technologies

Design and Synthesis of Magnetic Materials

  • Magnetic nanoparticles
  • Magnetic crystals, alloys, composites
  • Soft and hard magnetic materials
  • Permanent magnets
  • Diluted Magnetic Semiconductors
  • Magnetic Nanaparticles for recoverable photo catalysis

  • Computational tools (DFT, Monte-Carlo, etc)
  • Quantum Computation
